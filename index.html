Re-Visualizing Las Vegas:
Perspectives from the Chinese Diaspora

My thesis project is an interactive counter-map depicting the city of Las Vegas. A counter-map is defined as a map created to propose an alternate view that is not offered by traditional cartographic maps.


For this map, you will be able to click on icons, which will play a narrative from the Chinese residents I interviewed as a part of the project. While you listen to the narratives, the counter-map will change based on the themes of the narratives you hear. More icons may also appear after certain interactions. By interacting with my counter-map and listening to these narratives, I hope you will gain a new understanding and way of looking at Las Vegas as a city.
